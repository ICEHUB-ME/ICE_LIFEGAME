How to Play:

  • Click on cells to toggle them alive/dead
  • Drag to paint multiple cells at once
  • Play to start the simulation
  • Step to advance one generation manually
  • Random to generate a random starting pattern

Rules:

  • Any live cell with 2-3 neighbors survives
  • Any dead cell wit exactly 3 neighbors becomes alive
  • All other cells die or remain dead
